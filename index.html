Tutorial

WhatsOnTap Tutorial

Learn how to use our awesome software

Version | Licensed under GPL-3.0

Step 1: Navigate to "Edit a Menu"

  • In the Main Menu, click the "Edit a Menu" section.
  • You will be shortly redirected to the editor.

Step 2: Add in Your Details

  • Click on the "Add Item" option, and a form will appear.
  • Enter the title and description of the item to add to menu.
  • Click the "Save" button to add the item to the menu.
  • Repeat until your items are added then proceed to next step.

Step 3: Save Your Menu

  • Click the form and type a file name to save your menu as.
  • Click the "Save" button in the Editor. (The menu file will be downloaded to your computer.)
  • Go back to the main menu.

Step 4: Load Your Menu

  • Click "Load a Menu"
  • Locate and open your saved menu file on your computer / device.
  • That's it! Your menu is setup! Enjoy! (consider donating if this software helped you out!)

FAQs

Q: How do I exit full screen mode?

A: Depending on your browser this may vary, on Chrome to exit full screen press the "F11" hotkey or "F11" to re-enter full screen mode.

Q: Why is my menu not full screen?

A: If you are not in a full screen mode when you load a menu, your device / browser might not support full screen.

Q: Is there a mobile app available?

A: There is no mobile app available, but this web app is designed to be responsive to fit all devices.

Q: Why doesn't my menu doesn't fully fit my screen?

A: Depending on the screen size of your device, you may not be able to see all the items on your board. If you wish to see the max items you can fit on your board, zoom in or out with your browser. On Chrome, to zoom in - press and hold "CTRL" and the "+" symbol at the same time, or "CTRL" and the "-" to zoom out.

Q: How do I customize the look of the menu more?

A: Currently right now, there is only one default look of the menu. In the future, possible updates may allow you to customize / share menus built by other developers.

Q: How can I support this project?

A: You can support this project by supporting the developer (Jared Van Valkengoed) via a donation by clicking "Donate A Beer" button on the main screen. This will help ongoing development of this project that was built by him in his spare time.

Q: Can I modify this software?

A: Yes you can. This software is open-source & free. Just make sure you follow the GPL-3.0 license rules which you can find here.

Q: How do I exit the menu?

A: You can go back to main menu by pressing "Escape" key twice while inside of the menu. Or you can manually reload the page in your browser. Else just exit your browser manually as you would any other time.
